Knows how to forget!
But - could she teach - it?
'Tis the Art, most of all,
I should like to know -
Long, at it's Greek -
I - who pored - patient -
Rise - still the Dunce -
Gods used to know -
Mould my slow mind to this Comprehension -
Oddest of sciences - Book ever bore -
How to forget!
Ah, to attain it -
I would give you -
All other Lore -
